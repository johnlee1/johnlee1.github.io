Inspirations

Christie is a Communication Designer at Carnegie Mellon University with a minor in Human-Computer Interactions. She is originally from the Bay Area in California, but Pittsburgh is definitely growing on her.

Everyday Christie is learning more about the power of design and it excites her that through design there are endless opportunities to make the world a better place. More specifically, she is interested in creating meaningful experiences through digital interfaces that would positively impact society.

Christie wants to design to ease the inevitable frustrations of daily life and improve the quality of life for the less fortunate.

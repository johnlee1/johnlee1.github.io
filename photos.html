I think we just need to know that God loves us. We need to believe it. Everything else will come from that. I would have nothing to fear if I believe that God really loves me, that he really wants what is best for me, that everything he does is for his glory and my goodness.





Love makes us secure. Love makes us brave. Love makes us holy. Love makes us like Christ. The valleys, the wilderness, the mountain peaks and the highest heights. They are all carved by hands of love to make me into who he has called me to be. The man or woman that will bring him glory. He loves you. He loves me. He loves us.
